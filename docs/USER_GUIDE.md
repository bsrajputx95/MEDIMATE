# MediMate User Guide

Welcome to MediMate, your personal health and wellness companion. This guide will help you get started and make the most of all features.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Open the app** - Launch MediMate on your device
2. **Tap "Get Started"** - This will take you to the registration screen
3. **Enter your details**:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Your name
4. **Tap "Create Account"** - You'll be automatically logged in

### Guest Mode

Don't want to create an account? Tap "Continue as Guest" to explore the app with limited features.

### Onboarding

After registration, you'll go through a quick onboarding process:

1. **Personal Info** - Enter your gender and age
2. **Body Measurements** - Add your height and weight
3. **Blood Group** - Select your blood type
4. **Medical Conditions** - Select any conditions you have (or "None")

## Home Screen

The Home screen is your health dashboard, showing:

### Today's Overview

- **Steps** - Daily step count with trend indicator
- **Heart Rate** - Current resting heart rate
- **Water** - Water intake tracking
- **Calories** - Calories consumed today

### Quick Actions

- **Scan Food** - Open the camera to analyze food
- **Health Buddy** - Chat with AI health assistant

### Nutrition Tracking

- View your daily calorie progress
- See macro breakdown (Protein, Carbs, Fat)

### Today's Meals

- Track breakfast, lunch, snack, and dinner
- Tap any meal card to scan food

### Health Report

- **Overall Health Score** - 0-100 score based on your metrics
- **Recommendations** - Personalized diet suggestions
- **Tips** - Daily health tips

## CURA (Medication & Treatment)

CURA helps you manage your healthcare:

### Overview Tab

- Quick stats on appointments, medications, test reports, and doctors
- Quick actions for booking, consultations, and uploads
- Upcoming appointments
- Today's medications

### Appointments

1. **View all appointments** - See past and upcoming appointments
2. **Book new appointment**:
   - Tap the + button
   - Select a doctor
   - Choose date and time
   - Add notes
   - Confirm booking
3. **Cancel appointment** - Tap on appointment and select "Cancel"

### Medications

1. **View all medications** - See your medication schedule
2. **Mark as taken** - Tap the checkmark when you've taken a medication
3. **Set reminders** - Configure notification times

### Test Reports

- View all your lab test results
- See values and normal ranges
- Download reports

### Doctors

- Browse doctor directory
- View specialties and ratings
- Book appointments directly

### Treatment Plans

- View active treatment plans
- Track milestones and progress

## Healthyics (Health Analysis)

Healthyics provides AI-powered health insights:

### Health Tools

- **Food Scanner** - Analyze food nutrition instantly
- **Health Buddy** - Chat with AI about health questions

### Body Part Analysis

1. **Select a body part** - Choose from the grid (Heart, Kidney, etc.)
2. **Describe your issue** - Enter symptoms or concerns
3. **Get analysis** - Receive:
   - Nutrient deficiency suggestions
   - Lifestyle adjustment recommendations

### Adding Custom Body Parts

1. Tap "Add Part" button
2. Enter the body part name
3. It will appear in your grid

## Food Scanner

### Scanning Food

1. Open Food Scanner from Home or Healthyics
2. Point camera at your food
3. Tap to capture
4. View nutrition analysis:
   - Calories
   - Protein, Carbs, Fat
   - Serving size

### Manual Search

If scanning doesn't work, you can search for foods manually.

### Logging Meals

After scanning, tap "Add to Log" to record the meal.

## Notifications

### Managing Notifications

1. Go to Profile → Settings
2. Toggle notifications on/off for:
   - Appointments
   - Medications
   - Health alerts
   - Goals

### Medication Reminders

- Set specific times for medication reminders
- Receive push notifications when it's time
- Mark as taken directly from notification

## Troubleshooting

### App Not Loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Close and reopen the app
3. Restart your device

### Data Not Syncing

1. Pull down to refresh
2. Check your internet connection
3. Log out and log back in

### Notifications Not Working

1. Check device notification settings
2. Enable notifications in app settings
3. Restart the app
